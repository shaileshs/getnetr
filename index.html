How Netr AI Works

Our end-to-end solution seamlessly integrates with your existing camera infrastructure to provide continuous protection.

1

Camera Integration

Netr AI connects to your existing security camera network through a secure gateway device, requiring no camera replacements or upgrades.

2

Real-Time Monitoring

Our custom Vision AI model continuously analyzes camera feeds in real-time, detecting potential threats without storing or reviewing regular footage.

3

Threat Detection

When a weapon is detected, the system immediately flags the potential threat and captures relevant images while maintaining privacy compliance.

4

Instant Alerts

Administrators receive immediate notifications with location information, camera reference, and captured images through multiple channels.

Response Coordination

The system initiates your predetermined security protocols and can automatically alert law enforcement with critical information.
